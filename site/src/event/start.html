Step 1. Choose Your Webmaker Event

Webmaker Activities are fun and easy to organize. All you need is a few laptops, a wi-fi connection, and a group of eager participants! We’ll provide easy to follow instructions.

Remix Your School

Assemble a group of students and challenge them to hack their school’s website. Mozilla’s X-Ray Goggles tool makes it easy to alter the images and text on any web page. Replace the principal’s photo with a picture of Justin Beiber or make a funny edit to the school’s official motto and share the re-mixed version with a custom link.

  1. 2-10 participants
  2. 10-30 minutes
  3. Great for kids & teenagers

Remix Your School

Get a group of youth together to remix their schools’ web site. With our X-Ray Goggles tool, it's as easy as going to a school's website and changing some text or adding an image making Justin Bieber the new school mascot.

Music Video Mashup

Gather some aspiring musicians and directors to create an original video made from YouTube clips. Mozilla’s Popcorn Maker tool combines videos, music and text from around the web into custom mashups. Think Katy Perry’s latest video needs some dancing sloths? Add them in and share the mashup with a custom link.

  1. 5-20 participants
  2. 20-40 minutes
  3. Great for teenagers

Music Video Mashup

Participants will pick their favorite song from YouTube, lip-sync their own version on camera, and mash the two together to create their very own music video. Who knows? Maybe you'll discover the next YouTube sensation.

Private Eye

Bring together a group of web lovers and pull back the curtain to reveal who’s watching us online. Mozilla’s Lightbeam tool exposes the trackers who follow us around the Internet. Browse the web and see the hidden relationships between the pages you visit and the third parties that operate behind the scenes. Help others understand the importance of privacy on the web by sharing your custom visualization.

  1. 5-30 participants
  2. 10-20 minutes
  3. Great for teenagers & adults
