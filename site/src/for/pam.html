The Portland Art Museum (PAM) is calling makers of all ages to reinterpret its cultural works in new ways with the Online Collections Remix. Winning remixers will get a pair of tickets to the local art museum of their choice!

Here’s how to enter the PAM Online Collections Remix:

  1. Open the X-Ray Goggles bookmarklet on a Firefox browser

  2. Search for (openly-licensed) images on the PAM’s Online Collections using one of two themes - “Kinship” or “Transformation”

  3. Remix the PAM homepage with X-Ray Goggles by swapping in your new images!

  4. Finished? Share your remix and a short (tweetable!) summary of your curation decisions to PAM by 5:00pm PST on October 31, 2014 to win!

  5. Are you an educator who wants to share this activity with learners? Check out our Cultural Heritage Remixjam Teaching Kit.
