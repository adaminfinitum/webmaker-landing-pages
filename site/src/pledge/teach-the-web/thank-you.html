Welcome to Mozilla Webmaker!

Thank you for joining us and pledging to teach the Web. It’s going to be easier and more fun than you think!

To get started, just gather a group of interested people and choose from the three activities below.

  • Activity 1: YouTube Karaoke. Maybe you’ll discover the next YouTube sensation.
  • Activity 2: Hack Your High School. Get a group of youth together to remix their schools’ web site.
  • Activity 3: Private Eye. Pull back the curtain to see who’s watching us on the Web.

We are so glad you joined the Webmaker community and have taken the pledge to teach the Web. Feel free to email or tweet to @webmaker if you have any questions. We’re looking forward to seeing what you create!
